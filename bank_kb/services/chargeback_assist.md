# Chargeback Assist

## What it is
A service to help merchants reduce chargeback losses by organizing evidence, tracking deadlines, and guiding responses.

## Merchant value
- Fewer missed deadlines
- Higher win-rate on defendable disputes
- Better chargeback ratio stability

## What’s included (demo scope)
- Case prioritization by due date and amount
- Evidence checklist per dispute type
- Draft response packet (merchant reviews before submission)

## When to recommend
- Rising chargeback rate
- Many open cases due within 48 hours
