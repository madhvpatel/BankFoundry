# Settlement Cycle Options (T+0 / T+1 / T+2)

## What it is
A set of payout cycle configurations that determine how quickly settlements are paid out after a transaction date.

## Merchant value
- More predictable cashflow
- Better planning for inventory and operating expenses

## Typical options
- **Standard settlement** (e.g., T+1 / T+2 depending on rail)
- **Faster settlement** (where available)

## Eligibility (high level)
- Depends on merchant risk profile, KYC, dispute rates, and bank policy

## When to recommend
- Merchant asks about payout timing, settlement delays, or cashflow planning
- Merchant sees frequent `PENDING` settlements beyond expected dates
