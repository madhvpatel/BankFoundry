# Instant Settlement

## What it is
Instant settlement is an optional service that accelerates payout availability for eligible merchants.

## Merchant value
- Better cashflow predictability
- Reduced working-capital strain during peak sales periods

## Eligibility (high level)
- Subject to risk checks and internal policy
- Not available for all MCCs / risk profiles

## How it works
- Settlements are advanced on a faster cycle (or near-real-time) within configured limits.

## Onboarding steps
1. Confirm eligibility
2. Accept pricing/terms
3. Enable for the MID (may be per-terminal or per-channel)

## When to recommend
- Settlement delays are causing operational issues (supplier payments, inventory replenishment)
- High daily throughput with tight cash cycles
